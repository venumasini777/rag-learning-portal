Concatenation (The Merging)

The query and document are combined into a single sequence separated by a special token. No separate embeddings are generated, allowing the model to "see" both at once.

Why Cross-Encoders?

  • 01

    Beyond Vector Similarity

    Embeddings capture general topics but miss subtle intent. Cross-encoders capture full context interaction.

  • 02

    Full Attention Restored

    Unlike Bi-Encoders, every word in the query can attend to every word in the document simultaneously.

  • 03

    Precision for High-Risk

    Essential for medical, legal, or enterprise search where "similar" isn't good enoughrelevance must be exact.

The Speed vs. Accuracy Trade-off

Cross-encoders are significantly more accurate than Bi-encoders but computationally expensive. They are never used for full-corpus search; they are the Quality Specialist that works only on small candidate sets.
