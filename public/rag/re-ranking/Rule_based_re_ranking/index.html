Retriever Output (The Raw List)

The dense or keyword retriever returns the top-K chunks based purely on text similarity. At this stage, a document from 2018 might rank higher than one from 2024 simply due to keyword frequency.

Logic & Constraints

  • 01

    Fully Deterministic

    Unlike ML models, rules are fixed. If a rule says "Boost PDF files by 20%", it happens every single time, making it easy to debug and audit.

  • 02

    The Freshness Signal

    In production RAG, recent documentation is often more valuable. Rule-based ranking applies a "decay function" or a flat boost based on the 'created_at' date.

  • 03

    Governance & Trust

    Rules allow you to enforce security policies. You can demote or filter out "Untrusted" sources regardless of their semantic relevance.
